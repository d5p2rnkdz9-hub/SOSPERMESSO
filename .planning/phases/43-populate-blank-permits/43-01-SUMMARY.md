- **Structure:** Bold question as paragraph → answer paragraph → bullet list for details
- **Formatting:** Uses emoji for emphasis (warning, checkmark, arrow)
- **Lists:** Bulleted lists for enumeration, sometimes with bold first items
- **Warnings:** Inline with emoji prefix
- **Language:** Simple Italian, practical, step-by-step

### Standard Q&A Template for New Content

Based on the reference model, each permit should follow this structure:

1. **Che cos'è questo permesso?** — Brief definition, who it's for, when it's used
2. **Chi può chiederlo?** — Eligibility criteria as bullet list
3. **Come/dove si chiede?** — Application method (KIT postale vs personalmente in Questura)
4. **Che diritti mi dà?** — What the holder can do (work, healthcare, etc.)
5. **Quanto dura?** — Duration and renewal info
6. **Quando scade posso rinnovarlo?** — Renewal process or what happens at expiry
7. **Posso convertirlo in un altro permesso?** — Conversion options
