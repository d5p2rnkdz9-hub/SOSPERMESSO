Permit for Family for parent of Italian citizen (Art. 30)

Documents for first issuance Documents for renewal

What is the residence permit for parent of Italian citizen?

This is a specific residence permit for parents of minor Italian citizens.

How long does it last?

The permit is generally valid for 2 years

What requirements are needed?

To apply for this permit you must have a minor child of Italian nationality who resides in Italy and still have your parental responsibility.

Cohabitation is not necessary. You can have this permit for example even if the minor lives together with the other parent.

Where do you apply for this permit?

You must submit the application to the Police Headquarters (Questura) of the area where you live.

What documents do I need to apply?

  • Certificate showing that you are the parent of the Italian citizen
  • Certificate showing where your child lives in Italy
  • Document from the juvenile court showing that you still have parental responsibility
  • Valid passport

What can I do with this residence permit?

With this permit you can:

  • Work (self-employment or employed work) with a regular contract
  • Get residence in the Municipality where you live
  • Register with the National Health Service (SSN) and have a general practitioner
  • Travel in the Schengen area, if you have a valid passport

And when the permit expires?

Before it expires, you can

  • renew it until your child is a minor.
  • convert it to another permit (for example for work or study)
