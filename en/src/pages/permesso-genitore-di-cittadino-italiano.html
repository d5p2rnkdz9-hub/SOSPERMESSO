Permit for Parent of Italian citizen

Documents for first issuance Documents for renewal

What is the residence permit for parent of Italian citizen?

This is a specific residence permit for parents of minor Italian citizens.

How long does it last?

The permit generally has a validity of 2 years

What requirements are needed?

To be able to request this permit you must have a minor child of Italian nationality who resides in Italy and still have your parental responsibility.

Cohabitation is not necessary. You can have this permit for example even if the minor lives with the other parent.

Where do you apply for this permit?

Where do you apply for this permit?You must submit the application at the Police Headquarters (Questura) in the area where you live.

What documents do I need to apply?

  • Certificate showing that you are the parent of the Italian citizen
  • Certificate showing where your child lives in Italy
  • Document from the juvenile court showing that you can still act as a parent
  • Valid passport

What can I do with this residence permit?

What can I do with this residence permit?With this permit you can:

  • Work (self-employment or employed work) with a regular contract
  • Register your residence in the Municipality where you live
  • Register with the National Health Service (SSN) and have a family doctor
  • Travel in the Schengen area, if you have a valid passport

And when the permit expires?

And when the permit expires?Before it expires, you can

  • renew it as long as your child is a minor.
  • convert it to another permit (for example for work or study)
