Permit for Minor age

Documents for first issuance Documents for renewal

What is this permit?

The residence permit for minor age is given to foreign youth under 18 years old who are in Italy without parents or other close relatives. The permit is given even if you arrived without documents or irregularly.

Who can apply?

This permit can be requested simply for the fact of being a minor (being under 18 years old) and not having parents or other close relatives in Italy.

The application can be made by:

  • The minor themselves (with the help of a lawyer or operators from the center where they live)
  • The social services of the area
  • The guardian (the adult who takes legal care of the minor)

If the guardian has not yet been appointed by the court, the application can be made by the manager of the center where the minor lives.

Where do you apply for this permit?

You must submit the application to the Police Headquarters (Questura) of the city where the center in which you live is located.

What documents are needed?

  • 4 passport photos
  • 16 euro revenue stamp
  • Hospitality declaration with identity document of the center manager
  • Guardian's document appointed by the Juvenile Court (if already appointed) or declaration from the manager of the center where the minor lives

How long does this permit last?

The permit lasts until age 18 (until reaching legal age).

What happens when you turn 18?

Before turning 18, you can change your permit to other types of permits such as:

  • Work permit
  • Study permit
  • Job seeking permit
  • Family permit, if you have all the necessary requirements
  • Administrative continuation permit
