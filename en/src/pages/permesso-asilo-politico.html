Residence permit for political asylum (refugee status)

Documents for first issuance Documents for renewal

ğŸ“ What is this permit?

This is the permit given to those who flee their country because they risk persecution for their religion, political opinion, nationality, or because they belong to a certain ethnic or social group.

â“ How can I obtain it?

To get this permit you must:

  • Apply for international protection
  • Obtain a positive decision from the Territorial Commission or the Court
  • With this positive decision, go to the Police Headquarters (Questura) to apply for the permit

â±ï¸ How long does it last?

The permit lasts 5 years and can be renewed, usually automatically, for another 5 years.

ğŸ’¼ What can I do with this permit?

With this permit you can:

  • Register your residence in your municipality
  • Work legally (employed or self-employed)
  • Register with the National Health Service (SSN) and have a family doctor
  • Enroll in school, vocational training, or university
  • Receive financial and housing assistance
  • Apply for Italian citizenship after 5 years of legal residence (counted from the date you applied for protection)
  • Apply for family reunification with close relatives:
    • wife/husband
    • minor children
    • adult children if totally disabled
    • parents over 65 or dependent

You must go in person to the Police Headquarters (Questura) with the decision from the Commission (or Court) that recognized you as a refugee.
