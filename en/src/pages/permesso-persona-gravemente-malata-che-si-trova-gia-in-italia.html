Permit for Seriously ill person already in Italy

Documents for first issuance Documents for renewal

What is this permit?

The residence permit for medical treatment is given to foreigners who have very serious illnesses and who cannot be properly treated in their country.

If these people returned to their country, their health would worsen significantly ‚ö†Ô∏è or they could even die.

Who can apply?

You can apply for this permit if:

  • You have a very serious illness that cannot be properly treated in your country
  • You are already in Italy (even without a residence permit)
  • An Italian doctor (from a public hospital or one affiliated with the National Health Service) has confirmed that your illness is serious
  • If you returned to your country, your health would worsen significantly

Where do you apply for this permit?

Go to the Police Headquarters (Questura) of the area where you live and book an appointment at the Immigration Office.

The application is made with form 209 (not with the postal kit).

What documents do you need?

  • Valid passport (with your data, visas, stamps)
  • Medical certificate from a public hospital or SSN-affiliated doctor showing:
    • That you have a very serious illness
    • That you need treatment in Italy
    • That if you return to your country your health would worsen significantly
  • Hospitality declaration (where you live)
  • 4 passport photos

How much does it cost?

IT'S FREE! For this permit you do NOT pay:

  • The 30.46 euro for the electronic permit
  • The residence contribution

How long does this permit last?

  • Maximum 1 year (it can be less, depends on what the doctor says)
  • It can be renewed as long as you still have the serious illness and need treatment

What can you do with this permit?

With this permit you can:

  • Work (with a regular contract - self-employed or employed)
  • Enroll in the National Health Service (SSN) and have a general practitioner
  • Receive free medical care in public hospitals
  • Register with the civil registry of the Municipality where you live
  • Sign contracts for rent and insurance

‚ö†Ô∏è What you CANNOT do:

  • You cannot travel outside Italy - this permit is only valid in Italy

How to renew the permit?

To renew you must show:

  • New medical certificate confirming that you still have the serious illness
  • Hospital declaration stating that you must continue treatment in Italy
  • The same documents as the first application

Permit conversion

This permit can be changed to:

  • Work permit (if you find a contract and have all the requirements)
  • Family reasons permit (if you have Italian or European family)

Who can apply on your behalf?

If you are too ill, the application can be made by:

  • A family member (with power of attorney)
  • The manager of the hospital where you are hospitalized
  • Anyone who has an interest in helping you

Collecting the permit

  • You receive a receipt when you apply
  • The permit is ready within 60 days
  • The permit is paper-based (not electronic)
  • Another person with power of attorney can also collect it

Important difference

This permit is different from the medical treatment permit for those who come to Italy already with a visa for medical treatment. This is for people who are already in Italy and discover they have a serious illness that cannot be treated in their country.
