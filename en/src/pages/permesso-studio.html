Permit for Study

Information about the residence permit for Study

Documents for first issue Documents for renewal

What is this permit?

The residence permit for study purposes is given to foreign nationals who want to attend a study course, university program, or training course in Italy that lasts more than 3 months.

How do I apply for the permit?

The residence permit for study purposes is requested through the postal kit by attaching the required documents.

How long does this permit last?

This permit can last from six months up to one year. You can renew it to cover your entire study period.

What rights does it give me / what can I access?

With this permit you can:

  • Travel in Schengen area countries for less than three months with your passport
  • Work with a regular contract (employed work) for a maximum of 1040 hours per year
  • Register with the National Health Service (SSN) by paying a fee of at least 700 euros per year, or have private health insurance
  • Apply for family reunification, but only if you have enough money and suitable housing according to the law

Can I apply for this permit if I'm already in Italy?

Yes, you can apply if you already live in Italy and want to start or continue a study program.

To see if your residence permit can be converted to a study permit, take our test.

Can I renew it?

You must apply for renewal at the Police Headquarters (Questura) before your permit expires (or within 60 days), using the postal kit. You must include documents showing that you are still enrolled in your university or training course.

If you study at university, you can renew the permit for up to three years beyond the normal duration of your degree program. You can also renew it for a specialization or doctoral research.

What happens if I don't pass enough exams?

To renew your permit you must have passed:

  • At least one exam in your first university year
  • At least two exams in subsequent years
  • You cannot enroll beyond your third year past the normal duration

If you have documented serious health problems or other force majeure situations, you can renew your permit even if you passed only one exam after the first year.
