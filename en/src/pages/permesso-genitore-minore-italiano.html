To be able to request this permit you must:

You must submit the application at the Police Headquarters (Questura) in the area where you live.

Additional documents often requested:

  • Certificate showing where your child lives in Italy
  • Document from the juvenile court showing that you can still act as a parent
  • Any documentation of cohabitation or relationship with the minor

  • Travel in the Schengen area, if you have a valid passport
