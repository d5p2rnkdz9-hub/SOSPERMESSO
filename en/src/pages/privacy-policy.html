Privacy and more

Three important things you should know about the project

We only access essential information for navigation (IP address, browser type and version, operating system, pages visited and time spent, domain and address of the referring website). So browse with peace of mind: your data is safe.

We are not sexist

You will notice that on the site we use many words in the masculine form, but it's just a shortcut we use for simplicity: we want texts that are as short as possible and understandable by everyone. Permesso is committed to promoting diversity and inclusion of all people, regardless of how they identify.

We are not superhuman

We try to make our information as accurate as possible, but we might make mistakes, particularly in translations to other languages. Therefore:

  • Use this site to discover and learn, but before making final decisions we always recommend consulting a lawyer or legal expert.
  • You too can help make S.O.S. Permesso more reliable: if you notice an error, report it to us and we will correct it immediately
