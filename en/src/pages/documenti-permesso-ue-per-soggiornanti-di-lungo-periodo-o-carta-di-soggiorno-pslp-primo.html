What documents do I need to include in the Kit if I'm also applying for my spouse or a parent?

  • Forms 1 and 2: form 2 is only filled out if the family member has their own income
  • Photocopy of your passport and residence permit
  • Photocopy of your spouse's or parent's passport and residence permit
  • Certificate of criminal record and pending charges from the Prosecutor's Office
  • Residence certificate and family status certificate
  • Housing documents (property deed, rental contract or hospitality declaration)
  • Housing suitability certificate from the Municipality
  • Income documents (tax return, CUD, pay slips, employment contract, also from your family member if they contribute to family income)
  • Italian A2 level certificate

What documents do I need to include in the Kit if I'm also applying for my minor children?

  • Form 1
  • Photocopy of your passport and residence permit
  • Photocopy of your child's passport and residence permit
  • Residence certificate and family status certificate
  • Income documents (tax return, CUD, pay slips, employment contract)
  • Housing suitability certificate
  • School attendance certificate if the minor is required to attend school by law
  • Italian A2 level certificate (if the minor doesn't already have a middle school or high school diploma)

Remember that each family member over 14 years old must submit their own Kit. If your child is under 14, you can include them in your Kit.

Do I have to pay to submit the application with the postal Kit?

Yes, you must:

  • Purchase a revenue stamp of 16.00 euros
  • Pay the postal slip for the electronic residence permit of 130.46 euros (directly at the Post Office)
  • Pay 30.00 euros for the Kit shipping (directly at the Post Office)

What documents do I need to bring to Police Headquarters on the fingerprint appointment day?

At Police Headquarters (Questura) you must bring:

  • Your passport (check that it's valid)
  • Your residence permit
  • 4 passport photos
  • The letter they gave you at the Post Office with the appointment date at Police Headquarters
  • The Post Office receipt ("registered mail")
  • The receipt for the 130.46 euro postal slip payment
  • The originals of the documents you included as copies in the Kit
