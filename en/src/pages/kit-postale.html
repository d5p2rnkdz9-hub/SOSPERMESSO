This is a document you file when you host a foreign person for free in your home (for example a friend, a relative, someone you are helping). There is no rent payment.

You must go to the Police (Police Station or Police Headquarters (Questura)) within 48 hours of when the person arrives at your home. They will give you a form to fill out and then stamp the document.

This is a document you file when you rent a house or room to someone for payment. The person pays you rent to live in your property.
