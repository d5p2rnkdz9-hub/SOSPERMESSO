Permit for job seeking

Documents for first issuance Documents for renewal

ğŸ“ What is it?

The residence permit for job seeking allows you to stay in Italy even if you don't have or no longer have an employment contract. It is generally given:

  • To foreign workers who have lost their job and are looking for new employment
  • To unaccompanied foreign minors who have turned 18 without having stable employment
  • To foreign students who have graduated in Italy and have not yet found a job

â“ Who can apply?

You can apply for this permit if you are in one of these three situations:

  • You have a residence permit for self-employed or employed work but you cannot renew it because you lost your job (dismissal or resignation) or your self-employment ended
  • You have a study permit and you graduated in Italy (bachelor's, master's, PhD) but have not yet found work. In these cases it is advisable to first convert to job seeking and then to work. It is more difficult to go directly from a study permit to a work permit
  • You have a permit for minor age and you want a little more time to find a job

If you don't already have another residence permit, you cannot apply for a job seeking permit in Italy. To be clear: you cannot come to Italy without a visa or with a tourist visa, and then look for work with a job seeking permit.

ğŸ“‹ What do you need to do before applying for the permit?

1. Register at the Employment Center

  • Go to the nearest Employment Center
  • Declare yourself available to work (even in a different job than what you did before)
  • They will give you a registration certificate as a job seeker

2. Documents to register at the Employment Center:

  • Tax code
  • Valid residence permit (or expired but with a renewal receipt)
  • Valid identity document

ğŸ“ How do I apply for this permit?

  • If your permit is about to expire: send the application with the postal kit before the expiration date
  • If your permit has already expired: go directly to the Police Headquarters (Questura) and request an appointment

The total cost is 116.46 EUR:

  • 30.46 EUR for the electronic permit
  • 30 EUR to send the postal kit
  • 40 EUR for permit issuance
  • 16 EUR for the revenue stamp

The permit usually lasts 1 year and cannot be renewed.

With this permit you can:

  • Work (self-employed or employed)
  • Register with the National Health Service (SSN) and have a family doctor
  • Apply for residence

If you find a job: you can convert the permit into a work permit (self-employed or employed), with postal kit

If you don't find a job: before expiration, seek qualified legal assistance
