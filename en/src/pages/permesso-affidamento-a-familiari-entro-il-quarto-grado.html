Permit for Custody to relatives within the fourth degree

Documents for first issuance Documents for renewal

What is this permit?

This is a residence permit for family reasons given to unaccompanied foreign minors who arrived in Italy without their parents (MSNA) and have been entrusted to a relative within the fourth degree (brother/sister, uncle/aunt, cousin, grandparent...).

If you do not have relatives in Italy, you will be given a residence permit for unaccompanied foreign minor (MSNA).

The permit for family reasons is better because when you turn 18 it will be easier to convert it into another type of permit.

How long does this permit last?

It is valid until you turn 18.

What are the requirements to obtain it?

You can obtain this permit if:

  • You have been entrusted to a relative within the fourth degree (brother/sister, uncle/aunt, cousin, grandparent...) with a document from the Juvenile Court

Where do I apply for this permit?

You must apply directly at the Police Headquarters (Questura).

What documents do I need to apply?

When applying, you will need to bring:

  • Birth certificate or identity document (if you have one)
  • Custody or guardianship document issued by the Juvenile Court
  • Declaration from Social Services confirming they are taking care of you
  • Residence certificate or hospitality declaration from the community/family where you live

What can I do with this residence permit?

With this permit you can:

  • Enroll in school, vocational training, or university
  • Register with the National Health Service (SSN) and have a family doctor
  • Work with a regular contract (self-employed or employed), but only after age 16

What happens when I turn 18?

Before turning 18, you can convert your permit to:

  • Study permit (if you are enrolled in school or university)
  • Work permit (if you have a self-employed or employed work contract)
  • Job seeking permit (if you are looking for work)

Warning: you must apply to convert the permit BEFORE turning 18, otherwise the permit expires and you risk becoming irregular.
