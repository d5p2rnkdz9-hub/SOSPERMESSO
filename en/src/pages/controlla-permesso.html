Check if your residence permit is ready

Check your application status online

1) Check with the postal kit receipt

If you sent your application with the postal kit, you can check the status of your permit on the Immigration Portal website.

You will need:

  • The application number (you can find it on the receipt)
  • Your tax code

Go to Immigration Portal

2) Check with the application number

You can also use the application number directly, which you find on the postal kit receipt.

The number consists of:

  • Province code (e.g., MI for Milan, RM for Rome)
  • Numeric code

ğŸ“Š What do the different statuses mean?

  • In lavorazione (Processing): Your application is still being reviewed
  • Convocazione in Questura (Police Headquarters appointment): You need to go to the Police Headquarters (Questura) for an appointment
  • Pronto per il ritiro (Ready for pickup): Your permit is ready! Go to the Police Headquarters (Questura) to collect it
  • Spedito (Shipped): The permit has been shipped to your address

  • Check regularly: The status can change at any time
  • Keep the receipt: It is the document that proves you submitted your application

  • Always carry the receipt with you: Until you have the new permit, the receipt proves you are in legal status
