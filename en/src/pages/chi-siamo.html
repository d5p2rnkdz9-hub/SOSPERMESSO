About Us

We are lawyers and legal workers based in Turin. We work on the rights of foreign nationals in Italy. Many of us are members of ASGI (Association for Legal Studies on Immigration).

S.O.S. Permesso was conceived and created by Alberto Pasquero, lawyer and ASGI member, and was developed with the help of numerous people including:

  • Eleonora Celoria - Lawyer, ASGI member
  • Ilaria Ippolito, Legal worker, ASGI member
  • Serena Olivetti, Legal worker
  • Elena Tommasoni, Legal worker
  • Germana Marchese, Legal worker
