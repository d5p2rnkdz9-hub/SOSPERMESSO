Permit for Administrative continuation

Documents for first issuance Documents for renewal

What it is and how to apply

  • It is used to continue the assistance of social services even after reaching legal age.
  • You can make the application to the Juvenile Court by yourself or through your guardian.
  • A lawyer is not necessary to submit it.
  • It can be requested even if you did not have a residence permit for minor age.

The residence permit

If the Court approves the continuation, you can apply for a residence permit for administrative continuation.

  • This permit is valid until, at most, turning 21 years old.
  • It allows you to live legally in Italy, study, work and access SAI reception projects, even if you were not in reception before.

What must be indicated in the administrative continuation application?

What must be indicated in the administrative continuation application? To obtain administrative continuation, it is necessary that the applicant a) has undertaken a social integration path during minor age and b) upon reaching legal age, needs extended support aimed at the successful outcome of such path aimed at autonomy. In the application, you must briefly describe the path taken by the minor, from arrival in Italy to the moment of submitting the administrative continuation application, with particular attention to the social integration path undertaken during minor age and the prospects after reaching legal age, from the point of view of school, training, work integration, reception, residence permit regularization and any other aspects of social integration (volunteer activities, group activities, etc.).
