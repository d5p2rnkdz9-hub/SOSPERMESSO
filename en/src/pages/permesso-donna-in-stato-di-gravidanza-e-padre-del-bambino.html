Permit for Pregnant woman and father of the child

Documents for first issuance Documents for renewal

What is this permit?

The residence permit for medical treatment/pregnancy is given to foreign women who are expecting a baby or who have just given birth

This permit protects the mother and child ğŸ¤±, because pregnant women or those with small children can never be sent away from Italy.

Who can apply for it?

For the mother:

  • During pregnancy: until the birth of the child
  • After childbirth: for 6 months after birth

For the father:

  • If married to the mother: also during pregnancy
  • If not married: only after the birth of the child and after having recognized it
  • For 6 months after the birth of the child

How long does this permit last?

  • During pregnancy: until the birth of the child
  • After birth: 6 months

Where do you apply for this permit?

Go in person to the Police Headquarters (Questura) of the city where you live to make an appointment.
