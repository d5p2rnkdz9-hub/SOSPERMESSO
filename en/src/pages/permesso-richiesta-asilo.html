Permit for Asylum Application

Information about the residence permit for Asylum Application

Documents for first issue Documents for renewal

What is this permit?

The residence permit for asylum application (yellow permit) is a document given to those who have applied for international protection (asylum) in Italy and are still waiting for a final decision.

How do I apply for the permit?

First, you must go to the immigration office at the Police Headquarters (Questura) and request international protection: they will write down your application and take your fingerprints. After that, they will give you a residence permit for asylum application.

Anyone who applies for asylum has the right to this permit, which allows you to legally stay in Italy until the decision on your application.

How long does it last?

It lasts 6 months. But even if it expires, you continue to be legal until the final decision on your international protection application.

What rights does it give me?

  • Work with a regular contract (self-employment or employed work): you can sign an employment contract 60 days after obtaining the permit
  • Register with the National Health Service (SSN) and have a family doctor
  • Establish residence in the municipality where you live
  • Stay in Italy until the final decision (even if you appeal in court)
  • If you don't have enough money to support yourself, you can request to live in a reception center

Can I renew it?

The permit for asylum application is automatically renewed until the Territorial Commission decides on your international protection request. If you have already received a first negative response and you're wondering if you're still legal, it's a good idea to immediately seek specialized legal help.

Can I convert it to another permit?

No, this residence permit cannot be changed into other permits, except for the residence permit for family reasons.

What happens if my international protection application is rejected?

If the Territorial Commission says no to your application, you can appeal with the help of a lawyer. The permit for asylum application remains valid until the judge's decision.
